Game da MIQRA SCHOOL

Cibiyar Bincike da Ci gaban Ilimin Al-Qur'ani (Majidadi Institute for Qur’anic Research and Advancement).

Makarantar MIQRA makaranta ce ta musamman wadda ke haɗa zurfin ilimin addinin Musulunci da ilimin zamani domin gina cikakken ɗalibi mai dogaro da kai.

Tushen Makarantar

An kafa makarantar ne a shekarar 2023 ta hanyar tallafi da karamcin Injiniya Rabiu Musa Kwankwaso. Ya bayar da wani ɓangare na gidansa domin gudanar da wannan makaranta kyauta, tare da manufar tallafa wa ilimi da ci gaban al’umma.

Tsarin Karatunmu

  • Gajeren Lokaci: Ana koyar da matakan Primary zuwa SS3 cikin shekaru uku kacal.
  • Inganci: Muna tabbatar da ɗalibai sun fahimci darasinsu daki-daki.
  • Kyauta: Karatu gaba ɗaya kyauta ne don Allah da al'umma.

Sharuɗɗan Shiga

Muna karɓar ɗaliban da:

  • Suka haddace cikakken Alƙur’ani (Hizifi 60).
  • Suka yi nasara a tantancewa da jarabawar shiga.

Manufarmu (Mission)

Inshantar da ɗalibai su zama shugabanni nagari ta hanyar ilimi mai zurfi da tarbiyya irin ta musulunci.

Burinmu (Vision)

samar da ɗalibai masu cikakken ilimi da kyawawan ɗabi’u. Bayan kammala karatu, ɗalibai nagari suna samun guraben karatu (scholarship) a ƙasashen waje.
