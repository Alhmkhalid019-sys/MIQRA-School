Neman Gurbin Karatu

Komai KYAUTA ne don neman Yardar Allah

Sharuddan Shiga

Muna maraba da duk dalibin da yake son zurfafa iliminsa na Al-Qur'ani da na zamani. Ga manyan sharuddanmu:

  • 1. Haddar Al-Qur'ani: Dole dalibi ya kasance ya haddace Al-Qur'ani mai girma (Hizifi 60).
  • 2. Jarabawar Gwaji: Za a yi wa kowane dalibi gwajin Karatu da kuma Rubutu (Sato) kafin a ba shi gurbi.
  • 3. Tarbiyya: Ana bukatar dalibi mai tarbiyya da ladabi irin na musulunci.
Sani: Karatunmu baki daya KYAUTA (FREE) yake. Babu kudin form, babu kudin makaranta.
