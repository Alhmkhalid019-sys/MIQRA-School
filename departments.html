Tahfizul Qur'an

Kamar yadda yake a sharuɗɗanmu, kowane ɗalibi yana shiga ne da haddar Hizifi 60. A wannan sashen, muna mayar da hankali ne wajen muraja'a, inganta tajwidi, da kuma haddar matattara daban-daban na addini.

Arabic Study Class

Wannan sashen yana koyar da zurfin ilimin harshen Larabci (Nahawu da Sarfu), Fiqhu, Hadisi, da Tafsiri. Burinmu shi ne samar da matasa masu zurfin ilimin addini da za su iya kare martabar Musulunci.

Science Study Class

Muna koyar da darussan kimiyya kamar Physics, Chemistry, da Biology, tare da Computer Science (Programming & Web Dev). Muna horar da ɗalibai su zama kwararru a fannin fasaha (IT) da kimiyya.

Ci Gaban Karatu

Duk ɗalibin da ya nuna bajinta a sassanmu na Arabic ko Science, yana da damar samun guraben karatu zuwa ƙasashen waje domin ci gaba da karatunsa na jami'a.
